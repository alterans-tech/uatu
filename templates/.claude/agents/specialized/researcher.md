- "Research how authentication is implemented in this codebase"

## Research Output Format
When presenting research, provide:
- **Objective**: What was investigated
- **Methodology**: How research was conducted
- **Findings**: What was discovered
- **Evidence**: Supporting details and sources
- **Gaps**: What couldn't be determined
- **Recommendations**: Suggested next steps
- **Confidence**: Certainty level of findings
